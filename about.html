About PasswordChecker.in

PasswordChecker.in is a free, privacy-safe tool designed specifically for Indian users who need to create passwords that meet government portal requirements.

Why We Built This

Indian government portals (UIDAI, GSTN, Income Tax) have different password requirements. Users often struggle to create passwords that meet all these standards. We created this tool to solve that problem.

Our Promise

  • 100% Privacy Safe: Your password never leaves your browser. All checking happens locally on your device.
  • Always Free: No registration, no payment, no hidden costs.
  • No Data Collection: We don't store, track, or transmit your passwords.
  • Government Standards: We stay updated with the latest requirements from UIDAI, GSTN, and Income Tax portals.

How It Works

Our tool uses client-side JavaScript to analyze your password in real-time. It checks:

  • Length requirements
  • Character type requirements (uppercase, lowercase, numbers, special characters)
  • Common password detection
  • Government portal compliance (UIDAI, GSTN, Income Tax)
