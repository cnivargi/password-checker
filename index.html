‚ùì Frequently Asked Questions

Is my password stored anywhere?

No. All checks happen in your browser. Your password never leaves your device.

How does the leak check work?

We use k-Anonymity to check against Have I Been Pwned database. Only the first 5 characters of your password's hash are sent, ensuring complete privacy.

What makes a password "government compliant"?

Indian government portals require specific minimum standards for security. This tool checks against UIDAI, GSTN, and Income Tax portal requirements.

How often should I change my password?

Most government portals recommend changing passwords every 90 days for maximum security.

Can I use the generated password on multiple sites?

We recommend using unique passwords for each portal. However, our benchmark tool can show you which portals accept the same password.
