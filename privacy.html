1. Introduction

Welcome to PasswordChecker.in ("we," "our," or "us"). We are committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we handle information when you use our password strength checking tool.

We do not collect, store, or transmit your passwords. Our password checking tool operates entirely in your web browser using client-side JavaScript. Your password never leaves your device.

We do not collect:

  • Passwords you enter into our tool

  • Login credentials (we don't have accounts)
