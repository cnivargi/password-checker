Create a Strong and Compliant Password

  2. Mix character sets. Uppercase, lowercase, numbers, and symbols are mandatory.
  3. Avoid predictability. Skip names, dates, or simple sequences. Prefer unrelated words and random separators.

Hygiene Beyond the Password

  • Enable OTP/MFA. Treat OTP as a second barrier, not a substitute for strong passwords.
  • Secure context. Avoid public Wi-Fi. Use updated browsers and endpoint protection.
  • Monitor access. Track login alerts, unfamiliar devices, and failed attempts.
  • Limit privileges. Separate preparer and approver roles. Vault admin credentials.
